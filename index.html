STEVEN PAUL JOBS

The man who changed the world

Time line of Steve Job's life

  • 1955 - Born in San Fransisco, California, U.S.
  • 1968 - leaves home to attend Homestead High School along with Bill Fernandez who introduced jobs to Steve Wozniak, who would later become apples first employee.
  • 1972 - Enrolls at Reed College in Portland, Oregon.
  • 1974 - Returns home to his parent's house and starts looking for a job. He was soon hired by Atari, inc. in Los Gatos, California.
  • 1975 - Jobs and Wozniak attend meetings of the Homebrew Computer Club which was a stepping stone to the development and marketing of the first apple computer.
  • 1976 - Wozniak completes the basic design of the Apple computer and shows it to Jobs who suggests theys sell it.In April of that same year, Jobs, Wozniak, and administrative overseer Ronald Wayne founded Apple Computer Company (now called Apple Inc.) as a business partnership in Jobs's parents' Crist Drive home.
  • 1977 - Jobs and Wozniak introduced the Apple II at the West Coast Computer Faire.It is the first consumer product to have been sold by Apple Computer.The Apple II became one of the first highly successful mass-produced microcomputer products in the world.
  • 1978 - Jobs networth grow to 1 million $ at just the age of 23.
  • 1985 - Jobs resigns from apple and founds NeXT Inc.
  • 1986 - Jobs funded the spinout of The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar) from Lucasfilm's computer graphics division for the price of $10 million, $5 million of which was given to the company as capital and $5 million of which was paid to Lucasfilm for technology rights.
  • 1996 - Apple announces that it would buy NeXT for $427 million.The deal was finalized in February 1997, bringing Jobs back to the company he had cofounded.
  • 2011 - Steve jobs dies at the age of 56.
